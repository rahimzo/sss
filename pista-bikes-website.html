Our Story

At Pista Bikes UK, we're passionate about getting London cycling. Our expert team has decades of combined experience in bicycle sales, maintenance, and custom builds.

We pride ourselves on offering a curated selection of bikes perfect for city living, backed by comprehensive workshop services. Whether you're a seasoned cyclist or just starting out, we're here to help you find the perfect ride.

Our mission is simple: provide quality bikes, expert advice, and reliable service to keep London's cyclists rolling.
